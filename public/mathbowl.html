CNCM Math Bowl

To register for CNCM, you must do the following:

  • Complete the Google Form (mandatory)
  • Join our Discord!
  • Join our Remind

We use these platforms to inform our members about upcoming information (seminar and competition information), send hard problems, and many other things.

Discord

Discord is the primary platform that we use. Members can communicate with others who share their passion of math, and we also release PoTD's on Discord.

Google Form

We use Google Forms to collect our members emails, so that we can send them important infromation about our competitions.

Remind

Remind is used to remind our members about upcoming events that we are having. We also use Remind on days of competition, so that we can navigate with our members.
