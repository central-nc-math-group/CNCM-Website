2020 CNCM Math Bowl




CNCM Math Bowl is a bowl style math competition hosted by CNCM at Triangle Math and Science Academy. The 2020 CNCM Math Bowl competition will be hosted on May 23rd, 2020. Competitors must form teams of 4, and teams of members from different schools are allowed. The competition consists of 2 main parts:

  • Individual Round: This round consists of 20 questions (15 Multiple Choice and 5 Free-Response) and competitiors are given 40 minutes to complete this round.
  • The MathBowl Rounds: This is the main part of MathBowl. Teams face off in 1v1 matches consisting of 10 problems each.
