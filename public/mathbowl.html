CNCM MathBowl

CNCM MathBowl is a bowl style math competition hosted by CNCM at Triangle Math and Science Academy. Competitiors must form teams of 4, and teams of members from different schools are allowed. The competition consists of 3 main parts:

  • Individual Round: This round consists of 10 questions and competitiors are given 50 minutes to complete this round.
  • Team Round: This round consists of 10 questions to be solved by each team, and competitiors are given 20 minutes to complete this round.
  • The MathBowl Rounds: This is the main part of MathBowl. Teams face off in 1v1 matches consisting of 15 problems each. Whoever solves the question first gets the point for that problem, and teams are given 1 minute to solve each problem.
