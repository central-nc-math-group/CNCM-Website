Our Team

Here at CNCM, we strive to bring you the best burgers on the planet. We use only the best of natural ingredients while cooking your burgers. We also offer a variety of dishes that wil cater to any taste, whether you are white or indian or chinese.

Akshar Yeccherla

Akshar Yeccherla came up with the idea of CNCM after he wanted to provide opportunities to those who excel in math at school. Akshar is the current CNCM president and has an avid love for math. Akshar won the Central Carolina Chapter Countdown Round twice in a row, and placed in the Top 16 in State Mathcounts. Akshar is an AIME qualifier and has placed Top 50 in the National Spelling Bee.

Pranav Konda

Pranav Konda is the Technical Lead and was one of the first CNCM Members. As the Technical Lead, he writes and typesets problems and maintains the website. Pranav participates in many STEM competitions, such as the various Math and Science olympiads (where he qualified for AIME), FRC, and regional math competitions, as well as heading the CS department of InspireNC. In his free time, he likes to play Piano and Carnatic Flute.

Aditya Gupta

Aditya Gupta is the Organization Lead and a founding member of CNCM. As the Organization Lead, he helps with organizing problems, organizing events, and administering competitions. Along with CNCM, Aditya participates in AMC 10, DMM, SMC, and other STEM Competitions such as Science Olympiad, TSA, and FRC.

Kenan Hasanaliyev

At CNCM, we make it our goal to make thought-provoking problems every day. We have our own mini-competition called "Problem of the Day" and our members compete to see who can solve the most!

Minseok Park

Minseok Park was added as a Board member after being very active within CNCM events and activities. He has always wanted to give back to other students discovering the beauty of math, and is able to through CNCM. Minseok has always been interested in math, as he has participated in numerous contests such as Mathcounts, AMCs, the NC State Math Contest, and ARML. He placed 1st in NC Mathcounts, preceded by 20th at Nationals, and qualified for the USAJMO. He also enjoys other forms of competition, such as Science Olympiad and basketball.

Hari Desikan

Additionally, we have our own competitions, including CNCM Math Bowl and CNCM Regional Math Tournament at TMSA. We also send teams to other competitions including Duke Math Meet and Purple Comet.

Sushrit Pasumarthy

Sushrit Pasumarthy is a Board Member and the Co-Financial Lead for CNCM where he is tasked to secure funding and manage the finances of the organization to keep it running. He has participated in math competitions such as the AMC 10/12, Duke Math Meet, and the State Math Contest. He also participates in other STEM Competitions such as Technology Student Association and FIRST Robotics.

Srijan Oduru

Along with daily problems, we also provide competition preparation seminars and mock competitions for prominent competitions in the NC area and for global competitons as well.

Noah Sheinbaum

Noah Sheinbaum is the Chief Financial Officer and a co-founder of the Central NC Math Group. Noah has placed in the Top 20 in State MathCounts and has qualified for AIME through the AMC 10. He, alongside other CNCM members, participated in Duke Math Meet with the CNCM A team. Noah has competed in a variety of competitions, including but not limited to AMC 8, AMC 10, AIME, MathCounts, North Carolina State Math Contest, and Duke Math Meet. In addition to his competition experience, Noah has also set up a tutoring program at the Durham Rescue Mission to help educate underprivileged kids, primarily in mathematics.
