Who We Are

Cyclic National Competitive Math Group (CNCM) is a non-profit organization, whose activity is centered around a Discord server with over 800 members. We host multiple competitions, both in-person and now online.

Our Discord server has a wide array of features! Our monthly mini-competition, Problem of the Day (PoTD), has problems to challenge all of our users, and even has a fun competitive aspect. Lectures are released biweekly, and several PoTDs will be based on the topic, to help our members learn.

More casually, we also host study groups, work on math problems together, play games, and just chat!

Join our Discord server here, and check out our website at https://cncmath.org/ for more details about our organization.
