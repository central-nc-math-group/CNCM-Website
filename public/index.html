Cyclic National Competitive Math Group (CNCM) is a student-led math group that is dedicated to enriching our students' skills in competitive math. We try to accomplish this goal by releasing daily PoTDs (Problem of the Days), sending teams to competitions, and even hosting our own competitions. Our end goal is to make sure that students of all skill levels are learning more and more math and are feeling challenged beyond the level of their school's math class.

Hard Problems

At CNCM, we make it our goal to make thought-provoking problems every day. We have our own mini-competition called "Problem of the Day" and our members compete to see who can solve the most!

Competition Preparation

Along with daily problems, we also provide competition preparation seminars and mock competitions for prominent competitions in the NC area and for global competitons as well.

Competitions

Additionally, we have our own competitions, including CNCM Math Bowl and CNCM Regional Math Tournament at TMSA. We also send teams to other competitions including Duke Math Meet and Purple Comet.


Our group is full of people who love math and want to get better at it! We use Discord to communicate, and you can use the link found on the registration page to join. Discord is a free platform where users can interact in different channels depending on the topic.

Everyone is welcome to join, regardless of skill level!
