Problem of the Day

CNCM posts daily competitive math POTD's (Problem of the Day's), and all of these are based on important competition math topics. We try to vary the difficulty of the problems for all the varying skill levels in our group. After solving, we have discussion channels to discuss the problems and understand the different strategies of approaching the same answer. These dicussion channels can only be accessed after joining our server. In the widget below, you can see the last couple of POTD's, but to find the full list of POTD's you must join the server.

POTD Rules:

  • POTD's are released every day at 7:00 PM and remain valid to solve until 7:00 PM on the next day.
  • You may not discuss the current problem with others before solving it.
  • You have 5 attempts to solve the problem.
  • Each incorrect answer is -1 points, and correct answers are +5 points.
  • Direct Message "POTD Lord" to submit your answer.
  • POTD Leaderboard can be found below.
